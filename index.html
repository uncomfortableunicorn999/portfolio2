User Stories and User Personas

In this first project, I explored the interface of CVS self checkout, the people who use it, and the troubles they faced with the interface. I had my first experience creating user personas and user stories. I learned how to get in the minds of different users and empathize with their struggles. I myself have had a lot of troubles at CVS self checkout :)

Responsive Redesign

In this project, I took an existing website- Mountain Thunder, and redesigned it based on the design and usability principles learned in class. After seeing this website, and being utterly confused by how to navigate it and find any information, I knew this was a great site to redesign. Part of the project was thinking about the size and design of different elements when the page got resized, I thought on three different size, tablet, phone and desktop.

Iterative Design
